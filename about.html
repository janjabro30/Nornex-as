Our Story

Founded in 2014, Nornex began with a simple mission: to provide businesses with enterprise-grade IT solutions without the enterprise-grade complexity and cost.

Over the past decade, we've grown from a small team of IT enthusiasts into a full-service technology partner serving over 500 clients across various industries. Our growth has been driven by our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and staying ahead of technological trends.

Today, we offer comprehensive IT services including managed IT support, cybersecurity solutions, and custom web development. Our team of certified professionals brings together decades of combined experience to deliver solutions that drive real business results.
